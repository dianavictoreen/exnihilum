Ex Nihilum means “from nothing”, taken from the famous quote by ___ that says “Ex Nihilo nihil fit” meaning “from nothing comes nothing”. We aim to make something, something that creates a chain reaction that makes something else, and so on. Ex Nihilum Collective was co-founded by Diana Victoreen and Victoria Luzuriaga. Based in the eclectic heart of Brooklyn, New York, Ex Nihilum was born out of an urge to create a platform that merges a range of mediums within art, design, and literature while providing a seamlessly accessible platform for artists to exhibit their work. We extend our call for submissions far and wide to invite creatives of all walks of life and in every crevice of the universe to contribute to our collection of art. Ex Nihilum Collective’s motives are to connect with the arts community within Brooklyn and far beyond by moving away from the digital aspects of art and creating a tangible version that brings art into the real world. Our goal is to sprinkle our work and that of others all around, to make people stop for a second and appreciate something new and refreshing, and hopefully give them something to think about. We hope Ex Nihilum will be a bridging project between creatives, bringing people together, and spreading art-appreciation. We’re sprinkling the Ex Nihilum zine around New York City and beyond. We also have handmade t-shirts, totes, and other goodies on our online store for cheap or pay-what-you-wish. Feel free to contact us with your art for submission, thoughts on our project, love advice, random facts, recipe suggestions, or just to say hello!
